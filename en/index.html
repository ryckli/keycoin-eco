What Is KeyCoin?

KeyCoin (KEY) is an ERC20 token on Ethereum Mainnet that anchors 1:1 to daily electricity generation. It is the core currency of the Post-Economy Era — a decentralized abundance protocol designed to solve the structural fracture that AI creates in the traditional "labor-consumption-finance" cycle.

The Problem

AI can produce (output counted in GDP), but AI does not consume. When AI replaces white-collar workers at scale, GDP continues growing while consumption collapses. Citrini Research calls this "ghost GDP."

Traditional tools — rate cuts, QE, fiscal stimulus — all target "insufficient demand." But this time, the demand subject is disappearing.

Our Answer

Anchor money to electricity — the only universal value measure spanning both the digital and physical worlds. AI computing consumes electricity. Industrial production consumes electricity. Logistics consumes electricity. Every kilowatt-hour automatically generates distributable currency.
